About Us

BIT DURG is a premier institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Our achievements and IEEE associations set us apart in the realm of higher education. BIT provides the avenue for the development of an individual as well as the Nation in the field of professional education. It offers Doctoral Programmes (Ph.D), Management Programme (MBA) in the Full Time as well as Part Time mode, Computer Applications (MCA), Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, and Information Technology. Master of Engineering/ Technology courses is also being offered in all the disciplines.
